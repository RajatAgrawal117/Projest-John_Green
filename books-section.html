John Green's Books

  • Looking for Alaska

    Looking for Alaska is a young adult novel that tells the story of Miles Halter, a high school junior who transfers to Culver Creek Boarding School. There, he meets Alaska Young, a beautiful and mysterious girl who changes his life forever.

  • The Fault in Our Stars

    The Fault in Our Stars is a young adult novel that follows the story of Hazel Grace Lancaster, a teenage girl with cancer who falls in love with Augustus Waters, another cancer patient she meets at a support group. The novel explores themes of love, life, and death, and has become a popular bestseller.

  • Paper Towns

    Paper Towns is a young adult novel that follows the story of Quentin Jacobsen, a high school senior who becomes obsessed with his neighbor and classmate, Margo Roth Spiegelman, after she disappears following a night of pranks. Quentin sets out on a quest to find Margo and discovers more about himself and his own desires along the way.

  • Turtles All the Way Down

    Turtles All the Way Down is a young adult novel that follows the story of Aza Holmes, a 16-year-old girl with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) who sets out to investigate the disappearance of a local billionaire, Russell Pickett. Along the way, she reconnects with her childhood friend, Davis, and learns more about herself and her illness.
